Komaura is a one-woman press-on nail atelier in Jodhpur, India, founded by Komal Joshi in 2024.

Meet Komal —

The founder, the maker, the painter.

I trained in fine arts before falling in love with nail art on a trip to Paris in 2022. I came home certain that India deserved press-ons that felt like jewellery: light, intentional, beautifully packed, and made one set at a time.

Every set you order from Komaura passes through my hands. I size it, file it, paint it, seal it, and tuck it into tissue myself. There are no factories. There is only the atelier, the morning light, and your nails.

The Process

How a set is made.

01

Sized

You send sizing or use our complimentary kit. Each tip is matched to your nail bed.

02

Painted

Hand-painted in soft gel with a fine brush. Three coats, cured between each.

03

Sealed

Glass-finish top coat, hand-buffed at the cuticle line, and packed in tissue.
